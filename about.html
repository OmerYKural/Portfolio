Why Mechanical Engineering & Neuroscience?

I am drawn to mechanical engineering because it turns ideas into real-world solutions. Designing, building, and optimizing systems satisfies my desire to solve practical problems.

Neuroscience fascinates me because it explores the most complex system we know—the human brain. Understanding how people think, learn, and behave complements my technical interests and broadens my perspective.

Combining these fields, along with psychology, allows me to approach problems from both technical and human-centered angles. I aim to create solutions that are efficient and meaningful.
